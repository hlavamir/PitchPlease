# Cameo Q Spot 15 RGBW — Manual Ingest

*2026-07-10*

Ingested to document the pinspot fixtures at DMX addresses 6 and 10 in the vvvv patch.

## Key facts extracted

- **Product:** Cameo Q Spot 15 RGBW (CLQS15RGBW / CLQS15RGBWWH)
- **Type:** Compact 15W RGBW LED spot
- **LED:** 1× 15W RGBW Quad LED
- **DMX modes:** 2CH, 3CH-1, 3CH-2, 4CH, 9CH
- **4CH mode:** Ch1=R, Ch2=G, Ch3=B, Ch4=W
- **9CH mode:** Ch1=Dimmer, Ch2=Dimmer Fine, Ch3=Strobe, Ch4=R, Ch5=G, Ch6=B, Ch7=W, Ch8=Colour Macro, Ch9=Colour Temp
- **DMX connector:** XLR 3-pin in + out
- **Power:** 18W, 100–240V
- **RDM:** Supported
- Other: Master/Slave, Standalone, Music control, IR remote

## Additional clarification (from Miro)

- Fixtures at [0], [1], [2], [3] in the vvvv UV placement panel are **v2b strip IDs**, not DMX addresses.
- The Cameo Q Spot units appear as orange dots at positions [6] and [10] in the panel, corresponding to their DMX addresses.
